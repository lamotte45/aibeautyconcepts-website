Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2026

AI Beauty Concepts ("we", "our", or "us"), operated by Jacis Entertainment LLC, operates the AI Beauty Concepts mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Photos and Images
When you use the selfie try-on feature, your photo is temporarily processed on our secure servers to generate a style preview. We do not permanently store your photos. Images are deleted immediately after your preview is generated.

Usage Data
We may collect anonymous usage data such as which styles are viewed most frequently. This data contains no personally identifiable information.

Communications
When you chat with Zara, your text messages are processed to generate responses. These conversations are not stored permanently.

How We Use Your Information

  • Generate AI style previews
  • Provide personalized style recommendations
  • Improve our AI models and app experience
  • Process appointment bookings

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted. Photos are processed in real time and are not retained after your session ends.

Third Party Services

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use third party advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any data associated with your use of the app by contacting us at privacy@aibeautyconcepts.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
