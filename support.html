Support

We are here to help

How does the style preview work?

Our AI generates a photorealistic image showing what a hairstyle would look like. Upload a selfie to see it applied to your own photo using our custom trained image model.

How long does generation take?

Style previews typically generate in 5 to 10 seconds depending on your internet connection and the complexity of the style.

Are my photos stored?

No. Your photos are processed instantly and deleted immediately after your preview is generated. We do not retain any personal images on our servers.

How do I book an appointment?

After generating a style preview tap Book This Style to connect with available barbers and stylists in your area.

The app is not connecting. What do I do?

Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists contact us at support@aibeautyconcepts.com.

Who is Zara?

Zara is your AI beauty concierge. She uses artificial intelligence to understand your style preferences, recommend looks, and guide you through the app experience through natural conversation.

What styles are available?

We offer 50+ styles including low fades, mid fades, high fades, tapers, skin fades, box braids, cornrows, natural afros, silk press, beard designs, and many more.

Still need help?

Our team responds within 24 hours.
